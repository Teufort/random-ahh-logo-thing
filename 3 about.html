Why it was made

Teams at Teufort Tech needed a proprietary file archiver to the company. When they attempted to install 7zip on their computers, they couldn't get past the "sudo install /path/to/file.deb" part; and so, they spent 50 hours, tirelessly prompting to ChatGPT to create a new file archiver. It uses military grade AES256, certified 80 PLUS paper, and somehow passes IEEE 802.11 standards.
